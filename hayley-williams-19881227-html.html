Hayley Nichole Williams is an American singer, songwriter, musician and businesswoman best known as the lead singer, lead musician and keyboardist of the rock band Paramore. Born and raised in Mississippi, Williams moved to Franklin, Tennessee at the age of 13 shortly after her parents divorced in 2002. In 2004, she founded Paramore with Josh Farro, Zac Farro and Jeremy Davis. The group currently includes Hayley Williams, Zac Farro and Taylor York. The group released five studio albums: All We Know Is Falling, Riot !, Brand New Eyes, Paramore and After Laughter. Williams released his first solo single "Simmer" on January 22, 2020, and on the same day he announced that his debut studio album, Petals for Armor, will be released on May 8, 2020. Prior to that, the album has two EPs titled Petals for Armor I and II representing the first 2/3 of the album.
