Celebrities With Christmas Eve Birthdays: Ryan Seacrest &amp; More

Christmas Eve doubles as a birthday for stars like Ryan Seacrest, Louis Tomlinson and more! See which celebs were born on the holiday here.

The snow is falling. Trees are covered in colored lights. It’s ‘the most wonderful time of the year’…to have a birthday! What better time to be born than December 24th? After all, it means that you get two days of presents. While some might balk at the notion of sharing a birthday during the winter holidays, some celebs are blessed to have their special day fall right before Christmas.

Take Ryan Seacrest, for example. The ‘American Idol’ alum, ‘Live’ co-host, and busiest man in showbusiness was born on December 24, 1974. He gets to share a slice of birthday cake under the mistletoe.

Ryan shares a birthday with a handful of famous stars. From music icons like Ricky Martin and Louis Tomlinson to medical professionals like Dr. Anthony Fauci to pop stars like Louis Tomlinson, there are so many people who blow out the candles one day before they open up the presents under the Christmas tree.
