Is Trinity Rodman Related To Dennis Rodman?

Yes, Trinity Rodman is indeed related to Dennis Rodman. Trinity is the daughter of former professional basketball player Dennis Rodman.

She played college soccer at Washington State University before being selected as the second overall pick in the 2021 NWSL Draft by the Washington Spirit.

While Trinity’s father, Dennis Keith Rodman, is a retired American professional basketball player.

Renowned for his defensive skills and exceptional rebounding prowess, he is one of the greatest forward players in NBA history regarding securing rebounds.

The official NBA website describes him as possibly the finest rebounding forward in the league’s history.

Rodman’s career spanned from 1986 to 2000, during which he played for the Detroit Pistons, San Antonio Spurs, Chicago Bulls, Los Angeles Lakers, and Dallas Mavericks.
