Brian Krause Net Worth: $500 Thousand

Brian Krause is an American actor and screenwriter known for his work on the television show Charmed. He was born on February 1, 1969, in El Toro, California. Krause began his career in 1989 and studied at The Actors Workshop, El Toro High School, and Orange Coast College. He married Beth Bruce in 1996 and had one child before their divorce in 2000. Krause’s other television credits include Mad Men and The Closer, as well as a voice acting role in the video game L.A. Noire.
