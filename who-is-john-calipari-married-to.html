Who is John Calipari married to?

John Calipari, the renowned head coach of the NCAA Division 1 men's basketball team, the Kentucky Wildcats, has been married to his wife, Ellen Calipari, for nearly four decades.

Their enduring marriage has been a significant aspect of Coach Cal's personal life, contributing to his success and longevity in the world of college basketball. 

The Love Story

  • John and Ellen Calipari got married on June 22, 1986, just a few years after they met while working at the University of Kansas. Ellen, who grew up in rural Missouri, had previously been married to former NFL player Nolan Cromwell before meeting John at the University of Kansas.

Ellen Calipari: The Pillar of Support

  • Ellen is described as an adorable, witty, and skillful woman who has played a pivotal role in maintaining the Calipari household. She is actively involved in various activities, such as woodworking, sewing, baking, and painting, while also being an active presence on social media, particularly Instagram, where she shares content about her family and events.

  • Ellen is affectionately called "Mama Cal" by the Wildcats' fans and players, reflecting her nurturing and caring nature towards her family and her husband's players.

The Calipari Family

  • The couple has three children: two daughters, Erin and Megan, and a son, Brad. Erin, the oldest daughter, is an associate professor of pharmacology at the Vanderbilt University School of Medicine Basic Sciences, while Megan is a pastry chef, writer, photographer, and recipe developer at Earthly Provisions. Their son, Brad, is the Vanderbilt men’s basketball director of On-Court Player Development, following his time as a basketball player for the Kentucky Wildcats.

Professional and Personal Achievements

  • John Calipari has been a highly successful basketball coach, known for popularizing the Memphis Attack, a dribble drive motion offense, and achieving numerous coaching accolades throughout his career.

  • Ellen, on the other hand, is an accomplished and versatile individual, known for her woodworking skills and her dedication to maintaining their household and nurturing their children.

A Lasting Legacy

  • The Caliparis co-founded The Calipari Foundation, Inc., where Ellen serves as the charity's vice president. The foundation aims to raise funds for nonprofit organizations and implement specific programs to influence change, showcasing the couple's dedication to philanthropy and community service.

The enduring and loving marriage of John and Ellen Calipari has been a source of strength and support for both their family and the basketball community.

Their commitment to each other, their children, and their philanthropic endeavors exemplifies their dedication to making a positive impact on the world around them.

Ultimately, Ellen Calipari is not only John Calipari's devoted wife but also a talented, nurturing, and influential figure, contributing significantly to the success and well-being of their family and the broader community.
