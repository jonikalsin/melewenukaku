Biography

Danny Koker Jr was born in Cleveland, OH on January 5, 1964. Reality television personality known as Danny The Count, he is the star of History Channel’s Counting Cars. He is also the lead singer of the Las Vegas-based band Count’s 77. Counting Cars are the third spinoff of the wildly popular History Channel show Pawn Stars, which stars Rick Harrison, Richard Benjamin Harrison, Corey Harrison, and Chumlee. He had been running his restoration and customization business, Count’s Kustoms, for 15 years before being cast as the designing mastermind behind Counting Cars. He is a self-taught mechanic after growing up in Detroit, where his family worked for Ford.
