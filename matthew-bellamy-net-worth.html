Matthew Bellamy Net Worth: $40 Million

Matthew Bellamy is an English musician, singer-songwriter, and composer, best known as the lead vocalist and guitar player for the alternative rock band Muse. The band has released eight studio albums and has sold over 20 million albums worldwide.

Born in Cambridge, England in 1978, Bellamy began playing piano at six and guitar at 11. He formed his first band, Carnage Mayhem, at age 14 and later formed the band Muse in 1994.

Muse’s debut album “Showbiz” was released in 1999 and was certified Platinum by BPI. Their third album “Absolution” reached #1 in the UK and France. Their fourth album “Black Holes and Revelations” was released in 2006 and reached #1 in several countries. Muse has won a Grammy Award, five MTV Europe Music Awards, and an MTV Video Music Award.

Bellamy has a net worth of $40 million and was named the sexiest rocker of 2003 and 2004 by Cosmopolitan. He also holds the Guinness Book of World Records for most guitars smashed on a tour.

Outside of music, Bellamy has purchased several properties, including a $7 million home in LA’s Brentwood neighborhood previously owned by retired tennis star Pete Sampras and his actress wife Bridgette Wilson. He also bought Adam Corolla’s Malibu home for $3.6 million in 2014.

Bellamy was engaged to actress Kate Hudson in 2011 and they had a son together before ending their engagement in 2014. He married model Elle Evans in 2019 and they have a daughter together.
