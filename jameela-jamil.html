Jameela Jamil - Bio, Career, In Relation, Age, Net Worth, Facts

Table of Biography

Jameela Jamil is a famous English actress, model, advocate, activist, author, and presenter. Jameela Jamil is also the podcast host for “I Weigh!”. The multitalented personality is also very vocal about mental health and her struggles with mental health issues.

Early Life

Jameela Jamil was born on 25 February 1986 and her current age is 37 years old. Jameela hails from Hampstead, London, England, and her zodiac sign is Pisces. Talking about her family, Jameela’s father is a Pakistani whereas her mother is a Pakistani-British. Her father is Ali Jamil and her mother is Shireen Jamil.

Regarding her educational journey, Jameela attended Queen’s College, London. However, due to her accident, Jameela was unable to complete her A-Levels. Further, her full name is Jameela Alia Jamil.

Jameela Jamil – Health Issues

In an interview in the year 2015, Jameela shared that she had congenital hearing loss and labyrinthitis since birth. After several correctional operations, Jameela had 70% hearing ability in her left ear and 50% in her right ear. Likewise, she has shared that she was diagnosed with hypermobile Ehlers-Danlos syndrome at age 9. It is a genetic disorder that affects the connective tissue in the body.

Further, at age 12, Jameela was diagnosed with coeliac disease. Jameela experienced mercury poisoning at age 21. she shared that this happened due to mercury leakage from amalgam teeth fillings and improper removal of them.

Similarly, as a teenager, Jameela suffered from anorexia nervosa. Then at age 17, Jameela was struck by a car while running from a bee. As a result, she had several broken bones and also damaged her spine. Although Jameela was initially told that she might never walk again, she recovered with steroid treatment and physiotherapy. She then used a Zimmer frame to start walking again.

Jameela Jamil – Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Jameela Jamil is a famous actress, model, radio presenter, writer, and activist. Jameela started off her career on Channel 4. There, Jameela was the host of a pop culture series in the T4 strand. It was from the year 2009 to 2012.

Subsequently, Jameela worked as a radio host of The Official Chart. She was also a co-host of The Official Chart Update, for which she worked with Scott Mills on BBC Radio 1. Jameela became the 1st solo female presenter of the BBC Radio 1 chart show. She also appeared in the marvel movie, “She-Hulk” as main villain (Titania).

Then in the year 2016, she relocated to the United States from London. As an actress, Jameela is famous for her role as Tahani Al-Jamil in “The Good Place”. She is also famous for hosting the TBS late-night game show. She also gained fame as one of the judges of the reality competition show Legendary.

Talking about her career before her international fame, Jameela taught English to foreign students at the Callan School of English. She did so for 2 years in London. She also worked as a model scout as well as a model. Further, this talented personality also worked as a photographer, scout, and model agent for Premier Model Management.
