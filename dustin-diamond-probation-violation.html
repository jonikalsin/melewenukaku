Dustin Diamond, Screech from 'Saved by the Bell', Is in Jail

May 25, 2016 11:54 PM EDT

Dustin Diamond, the actor best known for his role as Screech on the early-90s’ sitcom Saved by the Bell, was arrested Wednesday in Wisconsin accused of violating his probation.

Two officials at the Ozaukee County Sheriff’s Office confirmed to TIME late Wednesday night that Diamond was in custody, but could not provide any more details.

Diamond, 39, served three months in jail earlier this year for his involvement in a bar fight that resulted in a stabbing on Christmas Day. Jurors found Diamond guilty of carrying a concealed weapon and disorderly conduct but not guilty of the felony charge of reckless endangerment.

In an interview earlier this month with Mario Lopez, Diamond described his experience behind bars as “daunting.”

“I want to put the tomfoolery and malarkey behind me,” he told Lopez. “Time for a change.”
