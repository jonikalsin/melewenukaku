Sofía Vergara Age & Early Life

Sofía Vergara was born on July 10, 1972, in Colombia as Sofa Margarita Vergara Vergara. Margarita Vergara de Vergara (mother) and Julio Enrique Vergara Robayo (father) are her parents. Her mother was a stay-at-home mom, while her father worked as a cattle rancher in the meat sector. When she was little, her siblings dubbed her Toti.

Sofia is a dual citizen. She is a dual citizen of Colombia and the United States. In addition, she is of Colombian heritage. Her schooling includes three years of dental studies at a university in Colombia. But she abandoned it to pursue a career in show business.

Sofía Vergara Height & Weight

Moving on to Sofa Vergara’s physical dimensions, she is 5 feet 7 inches (1.7 meters) tall and weighs 64 kg. Her hair is blonde, and her eyes are Hazel. Her physical measurements are 38-28-39 inches. Vergara enticing deep voice and distinct Colombian accent set her apart.

Sofía Vergara’s Net Worth

How much is Sofía Vergara’s net worth? According to Forbes, she is one of the highest-paid actors in Hollywood. According to Forbes (in 2015), she earns an average of $28.5 million each year. However, in 2016, she made more than that, totaling $43 million. Her net worth is now estimated to be over $180 million as of November 2023. She gets paid $500k for every episode of Modern Family. Sofia has her own apparel brand as well as a jewelry collection.

Career

Sofa Vergara began her career in the show industry at a young age. She was spotted by a photographer at the age of 17 and posed for a Pepsi ad. Vergara went to Miami to seek a modeling career after dropping out of college. She previously hosted various Colombian programs. Vergara made her television debut in an episode of “Baywatch” in 1999. She afterward appeared in small parts in various TV shows and films. But she didn’t give up and continued to wait for her chance.

She appeared as Lola Hernandez in the TV series “Hot Properties” in 2005. In addition, she participated in Tyler Perry’s films “Meet the Browns” in 2008 and “Madea Goes to Jail” in 2009. Furthermore, in 2009, she landed her first significant role in a television series when she joined the cast of “Modern Family.” She is still portraying Gloria Delgado-Pritchett in that series. She has appeared in films such as “The Three Stooges,” “Chef,” “Hot Pursuit,” “Machete Kills,” “The Emoji Movie,” and “The Con is On,” among others. Similarly, she appeared in TV shows such as “Fuego en la Sangre”, “The Knights of Prosperity”, and others.

Sofía Vergara Husband & Marriage

Who is Sofía Vergara’s husband? Sofa Vergara has been married twice before. She is now married to actor Joe Manganiello. The pair dated for six months before proposing on December 25, 2014. They first met at the White House Correspondents Dinner, when Joe was caught eyeing her. However, after the meeting, they fell in love and began dating. They married on November 21, 2015, in Palm Beach, Florida. They are currently living happily together and have formed an extraordinary friendship.

Her first marriage was to Joe Gonzalez, a high school lover. In September 1992, she gave birth to Joe’s son, Manolo Gonzalez-Ripoll Vergara. However, things did not work out with them, and they divorced in 1993. On July 10, 2012, a long time after her divorce, she became engaged to businessman Nicholas M. Loeb. But, once again, they were unable to form a deep relationship and called off their engagement.
